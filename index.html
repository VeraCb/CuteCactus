CARE AND HEALTH FOR
YOUR CACTUS

  • In cold time, add water once a month and during the summer do it when the soil is very dry.

  • Have a good drainage so that the cactus does not accumulate water.

  • Place your cactus or succulent in a location with indirect light.

  • Do not water more than necessary, as it can accumulate too much watter and the plant will not rot.

  • Do not expose to high temperatures, as succulent plants are not fans of extremes.
